This is my 1st course's exam. It requires a physical device to be connected. On the left, there's an emulator of the said device.

Only drawing is implemented, currently. No text support. The biggest pro of the approach I took, though, is that it could be added on the fly without changing the device's firmware.

To view the command log, you can open the dev console using Ctrl+Shift+J
